About K&A Painting Co.

Based in the heart of Krum, TX, K&A Painting provides a wide range of top-tier painting services for residential and commercial clients throughout Dallas and Collin Counties.

We have the in-house capabilities to handle everything from complex wallpaper removal and drywall patching to intricate trim painting and commercial office renovations. Our highly-trained technicians prioritize extreme attention to detail and absolute customer satisfaction in every aspect of their work.

Whether you need a single room refreshed or an entire commercial building painted inside and out, K&A Painting delivers results you can trust.
